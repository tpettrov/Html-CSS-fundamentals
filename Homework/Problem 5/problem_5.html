Headings and Paragraphs

  • Heading tags: <h1> - <h6>

    • < h1 > Heading 1 < /h1 >
    < h2 > Sub heading 2 < /h2 >
    < h3 > Sub heading 3 < /h3 >

  • Paragraph tags: <p>

    • < p > This is my first paragraph < /p >
    < p > This is my second paragraph < /p >

  • Sections: <p> and <span>

    • < div style="background: skyblue;" >
      This is a div
    < /div >
